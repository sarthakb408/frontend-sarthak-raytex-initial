About Titan Company Limited

Titan Company Limited (earlier known as Titan Industries Limited) – a joint venture between the Tamil Nadu Industrial Development Corporation (TIDCO) and Tata Group – commenced operations in 1987 under the name Titan Watches Limited. In 1994, Titan diversified into jewellery, and subsequently, into eyewear with Titan Eye Plus. In 2013, Titan entered the fragrances segment with SKINN.

Today, Titan Company – India’s unchallenged leader in watches, jewellery and eyewear – is credited with changing the face of all these industries.
